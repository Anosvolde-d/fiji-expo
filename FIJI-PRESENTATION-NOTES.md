# Fiji presentation notes

## Traditional outfits
In Fiji, the most recognisable traditional outfit for men is the **Sulu**, a wraparound cloth worn like a skirt. For women, a common traditional and ceremonial outfit is the **Sulu Jaba**, a long dress usually worn for formal events, church, and ceremonies.

## Kava tradition
The **Kava** tradition is one of the most important customs in Fiji. During a **Kava** ceremony, people prepare and share a drink made from the root of the yaqona plant, and it is used to welcome guests, show respect, and bring the community together.

## Currency
The currency used in Fiji is the **Fijian Dollar (FJD)**. A useful approximate conversion is **1 euro = 2.55 FJD**, although the exact exchange rate can change a little over time.

## Languages
The principal languages spoken in Fiji are **Fijian (iTaukei)**, **Fiji Hindi**, and **English**. These three languages reflect the country's cultural diversity and are used in everyday life, education, government, and tourism.

## Fiji stars
**Jerry Tuwai** is one of Fiji's most famous rugby players. He is the only rugby player to win two Olympic gold medals, in Rio 2016 and Tokyo 2020, and he is considered the heart of the current national team.

**Vijay Singh**, often called **"The Big Fijian"**, is one of the most successful golfers in history. He became World No. 1 and won three major championships, including The Masters.
